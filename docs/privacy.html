Privacy Policy

PixelPick does not collect any data.

Overview

PixelPick is a local utility that runs entirely on your device. It does not connect to the internet, does not collect personal information, and does not transmit any data to any server.

Data collection

PixelPick collects no data of any kind. No analytics, no telemetry, no crash reports, no usage statistics.

Screen access

PixelPick reads pixel colors from your screen in real time to display color values. This processing happens locally on your device and the data is never stored or transmitted.

Third-party services

PixelPick does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect data.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
